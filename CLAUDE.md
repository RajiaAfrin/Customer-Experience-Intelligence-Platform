# Project: Customer Experience Intelligence Platform

## Project Overview

This project aims to analyze customer reviews to understand the drivers of customer satisfaction and dissatisfaction, uncover hidden patterns in customer feedback, and translate those findings into actionable business recommendations.

This is intended to be a professional portfolio project that demonstrates strong analytical thinking, business understanding, technical judgment, and clear communication.

Do not treat this as a dashboard project.

Treat it as an end-to-end business analytics engagement.


# Business Question (Initial Hypothesis)

Initial hypothesis:

What factors drive customer satisfaction and dissatisfaction, and what hidden insights within customer feedback can help identify product quality or customer experience issues that deserve business attention?

This is only a starting point.

After reviewing the dataset, determine whether this is actually the best business question.

If the data suggests a more valuable business problem, recommend changing the project direction before implementation and explain why.

Never force the analysis to fit the original hypothesis.


# Dataset

Dataset:

Women's E-Commerce Clothing Reviews

Location:

data/raw/reviews_raw.csv

The dataset contains structured and unstructured information including customer ratings, recommendation status, demographic information, product metadata, and written customer reviews.

Assume nothing until the data has been inspected.
